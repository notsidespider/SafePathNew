# SafePath Marketing Strategy

## Overview
SafePath is a disguised emergency alert app targeting the general public — anyone who may find themselves in a dangerous or threatening situation and needs a discreet way to call for help.

## Target Audience
- General public across all age groups
- Students on university campuses
- Women and vulnerable individuals in unsafe environments
- People in high-risk professions or locations

## Key Value Proposition
SafePath looks like a calculator. No one knows it's an emergency app. That's the edge.

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Social Media
- Short demo videos on TikTok, Instagram, and X showing the disguise feature
- Target safety-conscious communities and student groups
- Hashtags: #SafePath #PersonalSafety #EmergencyApp #StaySafe

### 2. Campus Outreach
- Demo sessions at UENR and other Ghanaian universities
- Partner with student unions and welfare offices
- Flyers and word-of-mouth campaigns on campus

### 3. Community Partnerships
- Partner with NGOs focused on gender-based violence and personal safety
- Reach out to local police and community watch groups for endorsement

### 4. App Store Optimization (ASO)
- List on Google Play Store with keywords: emergency alert, safety app, SOS app
- Encourage early users to leave reviews

### 5. Word of Mouth
- Free app model to encourage organic sharing
- Referral incentives for users who onboard trusted contacts

## Success Metrics
- Number of downloads in first 3 months
- Active users sending test alerts
- Social media engagement and reach
- Partner organizations onboarded

## Budget
Low-cost strategy focused on organic growth, social media, and campus presence.
